## Features of the Plinko Live game demo

- **Classic Plinko board with visible pegs**  
  The ball drops row by row and randomly moves left or right, creating a realistic Plinko path through the peg grid.

- **Multiple risk levels**  
  Switch between **low**, **medium** and **high** risk to change the multiplier layout:
  - low risk: smoother multipliers focused around the middle slots;  
  - medium risk: more pronounced edges with higher payouts;  
  - high risk: very volatile edge multipliers with frequent low returns.

- **Auto play mode**  
  Launch auto mode and let the game drop balls one by one until you stop or your demo balance runs out. This makes it easy to observe how your bankroll behaves over many rounds.

- **Virtual balance and bet controls**  
  - Adjustable bet amount before each drop;  
  - Risk selector directly above the board;  
  - Balance label that shows how much you would hypothetically win or lose over time.

- **Mobile-friendly Plinko layout**  
  The **Plinko board**, controls and text sections adapt to smaller screen sizes, so the demo is usable on phones and tablets.

- **Pure front-end Plinko project**  
  The **free Plinko game** is implemented as a static HTML/JS app with no backend, no accounts and no tracking.
