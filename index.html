How this Plinko demo works

The game uses a fixed number of rows (10) and a set of multipliers for each bin, based on the selected risk level. When you drop the ball, the outcome is drawn according to a binomial probability distribution – central bins are more likely than the edges.

Risk level changes the multipliers: low risk gives smoother, more stable payouts; high risk creates huge multipliers on the edges but most hits will be in low or medium bins.

This demo is for educational and entertainment purposes only and does not represent a real-money gambling product.
