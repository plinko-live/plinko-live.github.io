How this Plinko demo works

The game uses a fixed number of rows (10). On every row the ball randomly goes left or right, bouncing on pegs. After the last row it lands in one of the slots at the bottom.

Risk changes multipliers per slot: low risk is smoother and closer to break-even, high risk adds huge edge multipliers but with more low hits in the middle.

Auto play repeats drops one by one until you stop it or run out of balance.
